Instant Ramen

Description

This ramen recipe will blow your mind! You can make it in 5 minutes with just the package and water.

Ingredients

  • 1 ramen package
  • 2 cups of water
  • *optional: 1 egg, scallions, pork, beef, sausage

Steps

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the ramen seasoning packet, veggie packet, and noodles.
  3. Add additional ingredients
  4. Let cook for 4-6 minutes depending on preference for noodle firmness.
  5. Enjoy!
