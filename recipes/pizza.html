Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe shows how to recreate Pizza Hut's new Detroit-style deep dish pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 lb of flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 oz yeast
  • 1 cup of mozzarella
  • 5 oz pepperoni
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, and yeast in a mixing bowl and let rest 30 minutes with plastic wrap on top.
  2. Once dough is rested, put a pinch of flour on a clean surface and roll dough thin.
  3. Spray square metal pan with olive oil and place dough in the pan.
  4. Sprinkle cheese generously on top of dough.
  5. Pour tomato sauce on top of cheese.
  6. Place pepperoni generously on top of tomato sauce.
  7. Preheat oven to 425℃ and place pizza into oven for 40-45 minutes.
  8. Cut pizza into slices and serve.
